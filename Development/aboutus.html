About

Nepal's first Brand Lifestyling Website

Hype Nepal is an ecommerce site for lifestyling and clothes. It was built on the vision of promoting and bringing genuine international brands and product of lifestyling to Nepal by founder and CEO Mr Saijeet Upadhyay. We sell limited products from only top brands as our CEO and founder believes in quality over quantity. The moto our system is top class service with best quality products.
